--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
PROTECTION LOGIC
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

    Daily P&L Calculation:

        daily_change = (current_value - daily_start_value) / daily_start_value

    Trigger Condition:

        if daily_change <= -2.0%:
            SET daily_loss_triggered = True
            BLOCK all trading
            LOG critical alert
            WAIT until next day

    Daily Reset:

        At first check after midnight:
            RESET daily_start_value = current_value
            CLEAR daily_loss_triggered flag
            RESUME normal trading

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
EXAMPLE SCENARIO
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

    Day 1, 9:00 AM  | Portfolio: $200.00 | Daily P&L: 0.00%  | TRADING
    Day 1, 11:00 AM | Portfolio: $198.00 | Daily P&L: -1.00% | TRADING
    Day 1, 1:00 PM  | Portfolio: $195.00 | Daily P&L: -2.50% | BLOCKED
    Day 1, 3:00 PM  | Portfolio: $197.00 | Daily P&L: -1.50% | STILL BLOCKED

    Day 2, 9:00 AM  | Portfolio: $197.00 | Daily P&L: 0.00%  | TRADING (reset)
