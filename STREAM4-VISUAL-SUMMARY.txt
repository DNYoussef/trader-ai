RC8: BIGEOMETRIC TRANSFORM BEHAVIOR
+-----------------------------------+
  Property: g_meta = g * |g|^(2k-1)

  k < 0.5:  exponent < 0  ->  DAMPENS  (scale < 1 for |g| > 1)
  k = 0.5:  exponent = 0  ->  IDENTITY (scale = 1)
  k > 0.5:  exponent > 0  ->  AMPLIFIES (scale > 1 for |g| > 1)

  NUMERICAL VERIFICATION:
  +--------+-------+-----------+-------+----------+----------+
  | k      | |g|   | exponent  | scale | g_meta   | Effect   |
  +--------+-------+-----------+-------+----------+----------+
  | 0.3    | 10.0  | -0.4      | 0.398 | 3.98     | DAMPEN   |
  | 0.5    | 10.0  | 0.0       | 1.000 | 10.00    | IDENTITY |
  | 0.7    | 10.0  | 0.4       | 2.512 | 25.12    | AMPLIFY  |
  +--------+-------+-----------+-------+----------+----------+
